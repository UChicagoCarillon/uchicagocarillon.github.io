of Carillonners

We offer tours twice a day, at 11:30 AM and 4:30 PM, four days a week Tuesday through Friday. Tours last half an hour and begin at the reception of Rockefeller Chapel, ending in the playing cabin at the top of the tower at 12 and 5 PM, respectively, at which point tour guides will begin their weekly recital.
