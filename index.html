UChicago Guild
of Carillonneurs

The Guild of Carillonists at UChicago is a student group led by Joey Brink, the University Carillonist, that plays the 72 bells on the Laura Spelman Rockefeller Memorial Carillon. We are 20 students both at the undergraduate and graduate levels. We hold auditions every fall quarter for students of the university with at least 3 years left in their program. Carillons are some of the largest instruments in the world by weight and are usually housed in a tower, although there exist traveling carillons that are mobile. They are incredibly varied in sound, age, structure, and behavior, with no carillon being equal to another. The instrument`s bells are usually cast together as part of a set and tuned prior to their installation. The sound produced by the carillon is often considered serious and somber, and it is widely portrayed in media as such. It has a great dynamic range and an extensive repertoire of pieces that are growing steadily each year.
